Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Spy Camera Detector ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

1. Information We Don't Collect

Spy Camera Detector is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user data to external servers.

2. Data Processing

All scanning and detection processes occur entirely on your device. Specifically:

  • Camera Access: Used solely for IR scanning and lens detection. Images are processed locally and never stored or transmitted.
  • Bluetooth Access: Used to detect nearby Bluetooth devices. No device information is stored or transmitted.
  • Location Services: Used only for magnetometer calibration and heading calculations. Location data is never stored or transmitted.
  • Magnetometer Data: Processed locally to detect magnetic field anomalies. No data is stored or transmitted.

3. Device Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Camera: To scan for hidden cameras using IR and lens detection
  • Bluetooth: To discover nearby Bluetooth-enabled devices
  • Location (When In Use): For magnetometer calibration only

You can revoke these permissions at any time through your device Settings. The app will continue to function with reduced capabilities.

The app integrates the following third-party services:

4.1 Google AdMob (For Free Users)

  • Used to display advertisements to non-premium users
  • May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
  • Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4.2 RevenueCat (Subscription Management)

  • Processes subscription purchases and manages premium status
  • Collects minimal data required for subscription validation
  • Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

5. Data Storage

  • Local Storage Only: App preferences and settings are stored locally on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: We do not maintain any cloud storage or databases
  • No User Accounts: The app does not require or support user accounts

6. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for users of all ages (4+) and contains no content requiring parental controls.

7. Data Security

Since all processing occurs on-device and no data is transmitted or stored externally, your information remains secure on your device. We recommend:

  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Using device-level security features (passcode, biometrics)
  • Only downloading the app from official app stores

8. Your Rights

As we do not collect personal data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete. All app data is under your direct control through your device.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. International Users

This app is available worldwide. All data processing occurs on your local device, regardless of your location. No data is transferred across borders.

11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

As we do not collect personal information, CCPA data sale and disclosure requirements do not apply. California residents have the same privacy protections as all other users.

12. GDPR Compliance (European Users)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • No personal data is collected or processed by us
  • Third-party services (AdMob, RevenueCat) operate under their own GDPR-compliant policies
  • You have full control over device permissions at all times
